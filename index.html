About $tarborn

Born and raised in the concrete crucible of Queens, New York City, $tarborn is more than a recording artist — he is a product of the grind. Coming up in one of hip-hop's most competitive boroughs forged a work ethic that runs bone-deep: every track written, produced, and engineered by his own hands.

A proud member of Dot Mobb, $tarborn carries the crew's legacy of authenticity and aggression into every verse. His roots in battle rap gave him surgical precision on the mic — the ability to dismantle opponents word by word, bar by bar. That same sharpness bleeds into his studio catalog: layered storytelling, raw street narratives, and undeniable cadence.

With multiple self-produced EPs, collaborative projects, and a growing body of work that spans theme songs for documentaries and podcasts, $tarborn is building an independent empire. He does not wait for doors to open — he builds his own.

The $$AB brand — Some Stars Are Built Born — says it all. Some arrive gifted. Some earn it through the fire. $tarborn is both. A star who was always destined, and never stopped working to prove it.
